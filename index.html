About IEDC CEK

The Innovation and Entrepreneurship Development Cell (IEDC) at College of Engineering Kallooppara is an active organization aiming to foster an innovation culture within our institution. IEDC provides venues for creative students to learn and collaborate, turning their innovative ideas into prototypes of viable products and services. We promote technology-based startup ventures from the college students and provide them with access to necessary resources. Additionally, we organize various workshops, webinars, talks, and competitions to help develop innovative ideas and encourage students to become entrepreneurs.
